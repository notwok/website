About Adam

I work with owners in both one-to-one and small group formats. The aim of my work is to improve the relationship you have with your dog by coaching you, as the owner, in ways to help you improve and develop your dog’s behaviour.

I hold behaviour and training sessions at various secure fields, or at a location of your choosing, as I aim to work at the best location for you and your dog, to support successful training.

I can help with problematic behaviours, which may have an emotional basis, such as aggression or separation anxiety. I can also work with you in a one-to-one coaching format to help with training goals, such as loose-lead walking and recall.

I look forward to helping you help your dog.

Adam Barber

FdSc BSc (Hons)

Services

Behaviour Consultations

Behaviour consultations may be needed if you are experiencing problematic or unwanted behaviours with your dog.

Problems frequently addressed include:

  • Separation-related behaviour problems
  • Dog-to-dog aggression
  • Aggression directed at people
  • Resource guarding
  • Excessive barking
  • Fears and phobias
  • Destructive behaviour

Dog Training

If you are looking to improve your dogs behaviours, such as recall or walking on a loose lead; or perhaps you have a new puppy or have adopted a rescue dog.

Aims that I frequently address with owners:

  • Walking on a loose lead
  • Not jumping up at people
  • How to play, and using this as a reward
  • Coming back when called/recall
  • Welcoming a rescue dog into your home
  • Teaching your dog to settle in a designated space
  • Getting your dog to settle in one spot
  • Pet dog scent work
  • Welcoming a puppy into your home
