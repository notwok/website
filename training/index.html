Dog Training

If you need support with your dog's problematic behaviour or would like assistance training your dog, I can help.

If you are looking to improve your dogs behaviours, such as recall or walking on a loose lead; or perhaps you have a new puppy or have adopted a rescue dog, and wish to help them settle into your home and teach them how to behave in your household, then a one-to-one training session will suit you best.

View pricing

Training session aims that I frequently address with owners:

  • Walking on a loose lead
  • Not jumping up at people
  • How to play, and using this as a reward
  • Coming back when called/recall
  • Welcoming a rescue dog into your home
  • Teaching your dog to settle in a designated space
  • Training to improve you and your dogs relationship such as getting your dog to settle in one spot
  • Pet dog scent work
  • Welcoming a puppy into your home home

When working with owners of puppies, I often cover topics such as:

  • House and toilet training
  • Play biting
  • Crate training and sleep routines
  • Learning to be left alone
  • Basic training for puppies - what to teach them and how to do it
  • Handling, such as grooming your dog and supporting stress-free vet visits
  • Preventing jumping up
  • Learning self-control and how to relax

Process

Click the titles below to read more about the process

  • Initial phone call

    An initial phone call to briefly discuss your training needs and confirm how our sessions will work.

  • Book a one-to-one

    Book a one-to-one coaching session via the website (requires a 50% deposit payment).

  • Goals and expectations

    Our first session will be slightly longer so I can find out exactly what your households goals and expectations are. The remaining 50% of the cost must be paid prior to the coaching session.

  • Summary of our work

    At the end of the session, I will provide a summary of our work and give you some incremental steps to help you towards your training goals.

  • Homework for you and your dog

    Finally, I'll always set some homework for you and your dog! This gives us clear objectives before our next session and helps assess the progress made.
